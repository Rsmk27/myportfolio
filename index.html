RSMK Technologies – IoT & Embedded Systems Projects by Srinivasa Manikanta

RSMK is an EEE engineering student and embedded systems developer based in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India. Projects span ESP32 IoT projects for beginners and advanced makers, including an agricultural rover with ESP32 for smart farming, a firefighter wearable IoT safety device, a solar-powered dewatering system aligned with PM-KUSUM, a single-axis solar tracker with LDR and Arduino, and ColorOhm — a resistor color code calculator app for Android. RSMK also provides resources for EEE students seeking IoT internships in India and embedded systems internship opportunities in Hyderabad and beyond.
